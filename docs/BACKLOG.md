**Goal:** Allow users to generate a printable or shareable image grid of their favorite finds (like a "fossil plate").

- [ ] **Export**: Generate High-Res PDF or JPG for printing.
- [ ] **Theme**: Support Monochrome/High-Contrast options for print readability.
